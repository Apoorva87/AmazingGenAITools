# Inference is the underserved skill

**Source**: inference.md
**Clusters**: [[Clusters/Inference Optimization|Inference Optimization]]

---

Many courses teach AI & LLMs but very few touch on inference. Efficient inference is one of the largest barriers to LLMs in production. Batching, memory management, concurrent sessions, GPU saturation, sharding, MIG, and parallelization are key concepts. There is no clear step-by-step guide on AI perf engineering — best bet is to study core model pieces and the hardware they run on.
